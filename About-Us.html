Message from the CEOs

Hello, we’re Erick James P. Rubin and Mark Lester G. Rasonabe, co-CEOs of WellLife360. Together, we’re passionate about promoting wellness and lifestyle improvements for everyone. Whether it’s guiding people towards healthier diets or inspiring them to stay active, our mission is to make wellness accessible, practical, and sustainable.

Recently, we’ve been working on this project, WellLife360, where we’ve created a platform focused on health and lifestyle transformation. Our site is designed to offer practical resources, tools, and support to help individuals create better habits for a healthier future. This project has been a wonderful way for us to combine our skills in health advocacy, leadership, and digital innovation.

With diverse backgrounds and shared dedication to wellness, we believe that meaningful change begins with education and simple, consistent actions. Our vision is to help individuals achieve their best selves, one step at a time.

What We Can Do for You?

  • Provide practical advice and tips for healthier living
  • Offer tools for creating sustainable wellness habits
  • Create engaging content to inspire lifestyle transformation
  • Simplify complex health and wellness concepts

We’re here to share ideas, support your journey, and work together to achieve your health and lifestyle goals. Let’s build a better, healthier you—together!
